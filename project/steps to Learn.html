Steps and Tips to Follow

  1. First, warm up, get comfortable with ice rink and skating environment.
  2. With little assistance, you need to stand on two skates.
  3. Feet should be apart from one another, and knees should be bent slightly to get the balance
  4. Do some basic gliding
  5. Practice falling
  6. Try stroking
  7. Encourage skaters to squat up and down without falling.
  8. Take little steps side-to-side, lifting one foot then the other.
  9. Learn to stop
  10. Place your arms in various positions like out to the side, overhead, doing windmill actions, clapping hands, etc.
  11. Lean forward while you skate
  12. Finally take a few steps forward.

Tip of the Day

(Just for Fun) If you still don't have confident to get on the ice, send your spouse, lover, friend, kids or anyone you know to learn skate. If they can learn successfully, It means you can survive too.
